Building Digital Connections, Empowering Community Voices

Welcome to Turtle Island Web Development, a team of passionate and experienced web developers dedicated to serving underserved communities. As an indigenous-led company, we're committed to empowering community organizations and amplifying their voices through innovative and inclusive digital solutions.

Our Mission

We believe that every community deserves a strong online presence, and we're dedicated to making that a reality. We partner with community organizations to create websites that inspire, educate, and connect. Our goal is to bridge the digital divide and foster a more inclusive online environment, especially for indigenous communities in Canada.

Our Approach

We take a community-centered approach to web development, listening to your needs and goals. Our team of experts will work closely with you to:

  • Design and develop websites that reflect your community's unique identity
  • Create accessible and user-friendly online experiences
  • Provide training and support to ensure your website thrives
