Building Digital Connections, Empowering Community Voices

Welcome to 7 Generations Digital, a team of passionate and experienced web developers dedicated to serving underserved communities. As an indigenous-led company, we're committed to empowering community organizations and amplifying their voices through innovative and inclusive digital solutions.

Our Mission

We believe that every community deserves a strong online presence, and we're dedicated to making that a reality. We partner with community organizations to create websites that inspire, educate, and connect. Our goal is to bridge the digital divide and foster a more inclusive online environment, especially for indigenous communities in Canada.

Our Approach

We take a community-centered approach to web development, listening to your needs and goals. Our team of experts will work closely with you to:

Web Design

Crafting visually stunning and user-friendly websites that effectively communicate your message and captivate your audience.

Web Development

Building fast, secure, and scalable websites that meet your unique needs and goals, using cutting-edge technologies and best practices.

Content Strategy

Developing a comprehensive plan to create, manage, and distribute valuable content that resonates with your audience and drives business results.

SEO

Optimizing your website and online presence to increase visibility, drive more traffic, and reach the top of search engine rankings.

Accessibility

Ensuring that your website is usable by everyone, including people with disabilities, by following Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1) and best practices.

Website Maintenance

Ongoing support and updates to keep your website secure, fast, and relevant, including regular backups, security checks, and performance monitoring.

Training & Support

Empowering you and your team with the skills and knowledge to manage and update your website, with personalized training and ongoing support.

Lianna Rice

Co-Founder Director of Operations, Strategy, And Web Development

“Atelihai ilonnasi! Hi everyone! Liannavunga- I'm Lianna, a Nunatsiavut Inuk from Labrador. My tech journey flourished with a web development internship, where I gained the skills to create intuitive and visually appealing web experiences. Co-founding MTN and being influenced by the intergenerational impact of the residential school system, which severed my connection to my Inuttitut language, I'm deeply committed to enhancing the accessibility of Indigenous languages. With a background in sciences, community work, and project management, along with deep values rooted in Indigenous rights, inclusion, equity, and accessibility, my work with MTN allows me to contribute meaningfully to future generations.”

Miranda Saddleback

Co-Founder Director of Community Engagement, Cultural Affairs, And Web Development

"Wâcîyê/Hello there! I'm Miranda Saddleback Nânâtêwikamik, from Maskwacis Samson four bands, rooted in Edmonton, all within the Treaty 6 Territory. Embracing my Indigenous Nêhiyawêwin Plains Cree heritage, I share its rich traditions as a cultural presenter & knowledge keeper. With a diverse background, including working in social work, & passion for advocacy, I collaborate at MTN, amplifying Indigenous voices through various initiatives (web dev’, community engagement, digital artistry, UX/UI design, & cultural revitalization). As custodian of the sacred History of Creation story, intertwined with Nêhiyawêwin knowledge, I am committed in preserving our cultural legacy for generations to come!"
